Welcome to Minesweeper!

The board is divided into a grid of cells. Some cells contain mines, while others are safe. The objective is to uncover all the safe cells and flag those that have mines without denotating a mine. If the player uncovers a mine, the game is lost. If the player uncovers a cell with no mine that is adjacent to a mine, the cell will contain a number which indicates the number of mines in the surrounding eight cells. The player must use these numbers to deduce the location of the mines.

Controls

Left click on an empty cell to reveal it.

Right click or long press an empty cell to flag it. Right click or long press a flagged cell to remove the flag.
